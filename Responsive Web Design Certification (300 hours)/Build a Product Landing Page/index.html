Health Concept

Why is QLY vegetarian full nutritious meal so good? QLY vegetarian diets nourish the five internal organs by the five colors of the food. The internal organs of the human body are interrelated.

In Chinese medicine, five lines are used to describe the five internal organs of the human body (liver heart, spleen, lung and kidney). Chinese medicine believes that five colors (cyan, red, yellow, white, black) enter the five internal organs, and five flavors (sour, sweet, bitter, spicy, salty) enter the five internal organs. Red enter the heart, white enter the lungs, black enter the kidney, green enter the liver, yellow enter the spleen.

Red enter the heart, red belongs to the "fire" in the five elements, in the five internal organs corresponding to the heart, more food can enhance the heart of the gas, improve the activity of cells in human tissue.
